    Discipline Playbook

    1. Identity first: Decide the kind of person you want to be. Ask: "What would a disciplined person do now?"
    2. Systems over goals: Make the next action obvious, easy, and time-bound. Use tiny commitments.
    3. Consistency beats intensity: Never miss twice. If you slip, do the smallest version today to keep the streak alive.
    4. Environment design: Remove friction, add cues. Layout your workout clothes, block distracting sites, set time windows.
    5. Track & reflect: Check off once per day. Review weekly: What helped? What hindered? Adjust.
